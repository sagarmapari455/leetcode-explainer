# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and inclusive environment for all members of our community, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge that our project will be a harassment-free experience for everyone, and we are dedicated to providing support for building the safest possible community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- ✅ Using welcoming and inclusive language
- ✅ Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- ✅ Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- ✅ Focusing on what is best for the community
- ✅ Showing empathy towards other community members
- ✅ Helping new contributors feel welcome
- ✅ Acknowledging and crediting contributions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- ❌ The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- ❌ Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- ❌ Public or private harassment
- ❌ Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- ❌ Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- ❌ Discrimination based on protected characteristics
- ❌ Threats of violence or violent language directed against another person

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they determine is inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to:

- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct
- Ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful
- Explain the reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of representing our community include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Consequences

Those who do not follow the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions, determined by members of the project's leadership.

Examples of enforcement:

1. **Warning** - A private written warning with clarification of the violation
2. **Temporary Ban** - Suspension from project participation for a specified period
3. **Permanent Ban** - Removal from the project and community
4. **Legal Action** - In cases of threats or illegal activity, appropriate legal measures

## Appeal

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, you may appeal to sagarmapari30@gmail.com with your evidence and explanation.
